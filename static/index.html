small but mighty

The A-hills home gym was born of necessity. The perfect garage wall was lined with junk for years, before the Covid-19 Pandemic of 2020 closed gyms around the world. Fortunately, Lowe's was still open. The quaranteam masked up and braved the hardware store at odd hours, worked tirelessly for three weekends straight, with improper tools, and no closed-toe footwear. Featuring artwork designed by a renowned artist, the peak of Mount Whitney and the graceful curves of the Mobious arch invite climbers to boulder on a short wall in a two-car garage. Memberships by invite only.
